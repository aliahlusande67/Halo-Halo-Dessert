Special Halo-Halo Ingredients

Ingredients for a Special Halo-Halo

A special halo-halo is made up of a variety of sweet and colorful ingredients. Here are some of the key components:

  • Shaved Ice
  • Leche Flan (Caramel Custard)
  • Ube Ice Cream
  • Sweetened Banana (Saging na saba)
  • Sweetened Jackfruit (Langka)
  • Red Beans (Adzuki beans)
  • Sweetened Coconut (Macapuno)
  • Pinipig (Crispy rice flakes)
  • Evaporated Milk
  • Sweetened Corn Kernels
